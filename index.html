about me

Thanks for stopping by - I'm Teisha Lou and I'm a U.K based, non-binary voiceover artist with over 5 years of acting experience. As a lifelong fan of TV and Video Games, I started voice acting in my early teens, after stumbling upon behind-the-scenes videos of the voice actors in my favourite games.

I thought it was the coolest job ever, and knew I wanted it it be MY job someday. I grabbed myself a Blue Snowball microphone and started auditioning for a ton of cool projects online. Eventually, I decided to pursue an education in acting and studied for a Diploma in Performing Arts in London. And now, equipped with a broadcast-quality home studio, I continue to work in various fields of voiceover, from Animation to Video Games to Audiodramas and many many more!

Directable, Versatile and endlessly passionate, I am always dedicated to providing high-quality voiceover tailored to your specific requirements.
